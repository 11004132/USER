Thirteen lines of culture

    The Shisanxing culture mainly cultivates rice based on agriculture, and lives in Ganlan House. The utensils include iron, silver, bronze, and gold. The burial method is bent-limb burial. At that time, the pottery was mainly red-brown and sand-filled pottery. Many unearthed beads, glass bracelets, glass ears, glass beads, and beads made of other materials have also found rare relics such as gold ornaments, gilt bronze bowls, silver tube ornaments, gold knife handles, copper bowls, silver bells, and gold coins. It can be inferred that the contact with other places is that the craftsmanship of the 13 pedestrians is quite developed, and they are good at making exquisitely decorated pottery pots.
