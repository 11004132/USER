Changbin Culture

    Changbin Culture is currently the oldest culture in Taiwan, mainly in the Taitung area, about 20,000 to 25,000 years. During this period, the main society of people was a group society of fishing, hunting and gathering. They not only hunted and gathered natural food for their livelihoods, but also Stone tools are made by beating, and more importantly, it is known to use fire.
